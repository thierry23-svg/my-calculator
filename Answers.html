Questions with answers :

1. What does "HTML" stand for?
A. HyperText Machine Language
B. HyperText Markup Language
C. HighText Markup Language
D. HyperTransfer Markup Language
Answer: B. HyperText Markup Language

2. Who is known as the father of computers?
A. Alan Turing
B. Charles Babbage
C. John von Neumann
D. Thomas Edison
Answer: B. Charles Babbage

3. What does "CPU" stand for?
A. Central Printing Unit
B. Control Processing Unit
C. Central Processing Unit
D. Computer Processing Unit
Answer: C. Central Processing Unit

4. What is the name of the first computer programmer?
A. Ada Lovelace
B. Grace Hopper
C. Margaret Hamilton
D. Tim Berners-Lee
Answer: A. Ada Lovelace

5. Which company created the iPhone?
A. Microsoft
B. Google
C. Apple
D. Samsung
Answer: C. Apple

6. What is the primary function of RAM in a computer?
A. To store permanent data
B. To execute arithmetic calculations
C. To temporarily store data for quick access
D. To process graphical data
Answer: C. To temporarily store data for quick access

7. Which programming language is known as the "language of the web"?
A. Python
B. C++

8. What does "IoT" stand for?
A. Internet of Technology

C. Internet of Things

Answer: C. Internet of Things

9. Who invented the World Wide Web?
A. Bill Gates
B. Steve Jobs
C. Tim Berners-Lee
D. Larry Page
Answer: C. Tim Berners-Lee

10. Which of the following is not an operating system?

B. Linux
